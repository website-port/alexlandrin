About Me

Brand Designer, Logo Designer, UI/UX Designer and Founder.

Hi, my name is Njonkoua Alex Landrin and I live in Germany. I have been passionate about drawing and graphic design since my childhood. Due to this passion, I have always wanted to learn more and showcase my skills since the age of 16. With graphic design being a vast field, I found my niche in logo design. I have learned from big and professional designers such as Chermayeff & Geismar & Haviv and The Futur. As a logo and brand designer, I help businesses create unique and appropriate logos for their brands. I consider myself a problem solver.

As a problem solver, I utilize an effective method for all businesses that come to me. This method consists of a theoretical part, a practical part, and a presentation. Theoretically, I learn more about the history of the existing or upcoming business, who their target audience is, and their vision. This way, I have a big overview and can start sorting concepts using a concept wheel. From these concepts, only one will fit the business. In conclusion, I implement the chosen concept.
