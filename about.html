About Me

Brand Designer, Logo Designer, UI/UX Designer and Founder.

I am delighted to introduce myself as Njonkoua Alex Landrin, currently residing in the vibrant landscapes of Germany. From the earliest days of my existence, a profound ardor for drawing and the intricate world of graphic design has coursed through my veins. This unwavering passion, kindled since the tender age of 16, has been my guiding star, propelling me towards a relentless pursuit of knowledge and a fervent desire to unveil my artistic prowess to the world. Amidst the sprawling canvas of graphic design, I have artfully woven my identity, finding solace and purpose in the captivating realms of logo design and the mesmerizing symphony of UI/UX creation. My journey has been illuminated by the wisdom and tutelage of illustrious maestros such as Chermayeff & Geismar & Haviv and The Futur. These luminaries have sculpted my craft, endowing me with the tools to craft logos that are not merely symbols, but embody the very essence of businesses' identities. Simultaneously, my journey has encompassed the intricate dance of UI/UX design, where I orchestrate seamless digital experiences that resonate with users on a profound level. In my creative odyssey, I refrain from merely donning the mantle of a designer; I instead assume the mantle of a problem solver. Through my artistic lens, I decipher the intricacies of each challenge, unraveling them thread by thread until I unveil elegant, innovative solutions that leave an indelible mark on brands and users alike.
